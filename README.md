# Dirty Cafe Analysis

## One-Line Summary
Performed data cleaning and exploratory data analysis (EDA) on the Dirty Cafe dataset to identify the highest-earning months and best-selling items.

## Overview
This project involves analyzing the sales data of Dirty Cafe to uncover key insights such as which months generate the most revenue, which items are sold the most, and which products contribute the highest revenue. The dataset required cleaning due to duplicates and null values before meaningful analysis could be performed.

## Problem Statement
Dirty Cafe wants to understand its sales trends to optimize inventory and marketing strategies. Specifically, the cafe wants to know:
- In which months the cafe earns the most revenue
- Which items are sold the most
- Which items generate the highest revenue

## Dataset
- **File Name:** `dirty_cafe_sales.csv`  
- **Number of Rows:** 10,000  
- **Number of Columns:** 8  
- **Key Issues:** Duplicated entries and missing/null values

## Tools and Technology
- **Programming Language:** Python  
- **Libraries:** Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn  
- **Platform:** Jupyter Notebook  

## Methodology
1. **Data Cleaning**
   - Checked and removed duplicate records
   - Handled missing/null values
   - Adjusted price according to item quantity and spending
2. **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**
   - Calculated monthly sales totals
   - Analyzed sales by item
   - Computed revenue contribution per item
3. **Data Visualization**
   - Line charts for monthly sales trends
   - Bar charts for item-wise sales and revenue
   - Heatmaps for correlations (if applicable)

## Key Insights
- **Highest Earning Month:** June  
- **Most Sold Item:** Cake  
- **Item Generating Most Revenue:** Salad  
- **Month with Most Earnings:** October  

## Dashboard / Visualizations
- **Bar Chart:** Monthly revenue trends
- **PieChart:** Revenue by item  
- **Bar Chart:** Number of items sold  



## Results and Conclusion
After cleaning and analyzing the Dirty Cafe sales data:
- June is the highest-earning month  
- Cake is the most sold item  
- Salad generates the highest revenue  
- October also shows peak earnings, highlighting seasonal trends  
These insights can help Dirty Cafe make informed decisions regarding inventory management, promotions, and sales strategies.
